Payments in a snap

SnapScan began as a small local start up with big dreams. Our team of passionate problem solvers wanted to find a faster, easier and safer way to pay without the fuss of handling cash and credit cards. And we did it!

MTN Business App of the Year 2013

We’re really proud to have won the MTN Business App of the Year shortly after we launched.

50 000 Merchants Nationwide

SnapScan has become a household name with one of the largest networks of merchants in the country.

1 million downloads to date

Users love us too! Our numbers keep growing with more and more happy snappers choosing SnapScan as the fastest way to pay.

In 2013, SnapScan launched the first mobile payment app in South Africa. By combining our can-do attitude and fintech smarts with Standard Bank’s impressive financial infrastructure and expertise, SnapScan has been changing the way South Africans pay and get paid - one snap at a time.

Problem Solving

Our team of passionate problem solvers continue to find faster, easier and safer ways to transact without the fuss of handling cash and credit cards. We seek to ensure that people save time and have the best payment experience possible. We strive to remove unnecessary barriers, complex fee structures, and time consuming admin and provide an innovative and streamlined solution for businesses and app users. Our dedicated team are always happy to assist and we are constantly evolving - as we continue to find innovative ways to pay in a snap.
